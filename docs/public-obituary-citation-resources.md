# Public Obituary Citation Resources

This repository backs FinalNotes, a public archive of source-backed obituary comics, obituary stories, and obituary-writing resources.

Use these links when citing the project from GitHub, library guides, journalism lessons, obituary-writing resources, or source-request profiles.

## Primary Obituary Story Targets

- [Obituary stories and articles](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-stories/) - the main archive index for source-backed visual obituary stories.
- [Obituary articles as reported life stories](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-articles/) - a guide to obituary articles, feature obituaries, and visual life-story examples.
- [What are obituary comics?](https://www.finalnotes.page/what-are-obituary-comics/) - an explainer for visual obituaries, comics biography, and source-backed sequential storytelling after death.

## Writing And Research Targets

- [How to write an obituary story](https://www.finalnotes.page/how-to-write-an-obituary-story/) - a practical guide for turning facts, records, and memories into a sourced life story.
- [Obituary research guide](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-research-guide/) - a guide for genealogy, library, newspaper, archive, and family-history obituary research.
- [Obituary story worksheet](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-story-worksheet/) - a printable planning worksheet for source notes, privacy checks, and story details.
- [Obituary lesson plan](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-lesson-plan/) - a classroom resource for journalism, ELA, media writing, and family-history assignments.

## Safety And Format Targets

- [What not to include in an obituary](https://www.finalnotes.page/what-not-to-include-in-an-obituary/) - privacy and identity-risk guidance before publishing a public obituary.
- [Fake obituary sites](https://www.finalnotes.page/fake-obituary-sites/) - a checklist for suspicious obituary pages, copied notices, and fake donation links.
- [Obituary vs death notice](https://www.finalnotes.page/obituary-vs-death-notice/) - a format explainer for newspaper notices, funeral-home pages, and fuller obituary stories.

## Source Context

- [Press and source-request resources](https://www.finalnotes.page/press/) - canonical citation targets, story angles, source topics, and current archive context for editors, journalists, librarians, and educators.
- [Editorial method](https://www.finalnotes.page/about/) - source standards, subject selection, and publishing notes for the archive.
